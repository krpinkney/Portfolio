LET ME INTRODUCE MYSELF

My name is Kristina Pinkney. I’m currently an 18 year old design student at Massey University’s Colledge of Creative Arts in Wellington, New Zealand.

I have a love for design, photography, filmmaking and all things visually appealing, as well as music (especially the indie kind).

When I’m not hidden away in my room doing some video editing, watching a good tv show, or listening to a bit of music, you can probably find me out and about, exploring new places and filming for an online video project called ‘Document Your Life’ or petting the local cat.
